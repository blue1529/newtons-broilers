3. Delivery Terms

3.1. Delivery times provided are estimates and may vary based on location, traffic conditions, and product availability.

3.2. Free delivery is offered for orders above MWK 30,000 within Blantyre city limits. Delivery charges apply for orders below this threshold and for locations outside Blantyre.

3.3. Customers must provide accurate delivery addresses. Additional charges may apply for address corrections or failed delivery attempts.

3.4. For slaughtered products requiring refrigeration, delivery must be accepted within 15 minutes of arrival to ensure product quality.

3.5. We are not responsible for delivery delays caused by circumstances beyond our control (e.g., extreme weather, road closures, strikes).

Important:

Live poultry must be collected from the delivery vehicle immediately upon arrival. We cannot hold live animals in vehicles for extended periods.

4. Payment Terms

4.1. We accept the following payment methods: cash on delivery, bank transfers, and mobile money (Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba).

4.2. Full payment must be received before delivery unless credit terms have been explicitly agreed upon in writing.

4.3. For bank transfers, orders will be processed only after funds are confirmed in our account.

4.4. Bulk orders may require a 50% deposit at the time of order confirmation, with the balance due before delivery.

4.5. Prices are subject to change without notice, but confirmed orders will be honored at the agreed price.

5. Returns & Refunds

5.1. Live poultry sales are final once collected from our farm or received from our delivery vehicle.

5.2. Slaughtered products may be returned within 2 hours of delivery if quality issues are identified (e.g., spoilage, incorrect product).

5.3. To initiate a return, contact us immediately at +265 996 893 890 with photos of the product and your order details.

5.4. Approved refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days through the original payment method.

5.5. Products must be returned in their original packaging and condition for refund consideration.

5.6. We do not offer refunds for changes of mind or incorrect orders placed by customers.
